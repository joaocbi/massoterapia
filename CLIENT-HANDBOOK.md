# Flow Terapias - Client Handbook

This guide explains what Flow Terapias offers, the benefits of each massage, and how booking and payment work.

## 1) What the site offers

- Fast online booking in a few minutes.
- Clear service menu with duration and price.
- Real-time date and time availability.
- Multiple payment options (Pix, credit card, debit card).
- Booking confirmation summary before final submission.
- WhatsApp-ready communication flow after booking.
- Secure payment flow with Mercado Pago for supported methods.

## 3) How booking works

1. Open the Flow Terapias website.
2. Go to the booking section.
3. Fill in your personal and contact details.
4. Choose massage type, date, and available time.
5. Choose your payment method.
6. Review your booking summary.
7. Confirm the booking.

After confirmation, your appointment is registered and appears in the business management panel.

## 4) How payment works

- **Pix, Credit Card, or Debit Card**: the system can request prepayment and generate a Mercado Pago checkout flow when configured.
- **Payment status tracking**: payment can be tracked as pending or paid by integration and admin management.
- **Security**: payments are processed by official payment infrastructure.

## 5) Availability rules

- Only active business days can be booked.
- Blocked dates cannot be selected.
- Occupied or disabled times are shown as unavailable.
- The business can adjust services, schedules, and methods at any time.

## 6) Best experience tips

- Book in advance to get your preferred time.
- Double-check your WhatsApp number before confirming.
- Arrive hydrated and with a light routine on the day of the session.
- If needed, contact the business on WhatsApp for adjustments.
